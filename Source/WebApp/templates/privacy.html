Privacy Policy

What kind of information do we collect?

This policy describes the information we receive for the functionality of the site. We collect only information you provide. This data includes the website usernames, passwords, and profile picture uploads. Any password stored on this site is encrypted and never stored in plain-text. We do not use cookies.

Use of information

This information is used for the functionality of the site, allowing users to launch rockets and tag rocket launch videos as their own. Information that is collected is not shared.

Deletion of information

If a user wants their information to be deleted, they may use the delete account tool in the My Account page.
